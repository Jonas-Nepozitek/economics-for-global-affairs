Per the above scatterplot, most sectors showed very little change in the RCA in these 25 years. If anything, most non-resource sectors (marked as circles), showed marginal declines in the RCA (shown in red). However, there are a handful of sectors in which the increase in RCA is more noticeable. Of these, only about half are non-resource/non-mineral sectors. The other half of sectors in which Botswana's comparative advantage markedly increased were in mineral sectors (although the two largest declines were also in mining industries). Overall, the data shows mixed results in Botswana's comparative advantage gains in non-resource sectors. While a handful showed major improvement, the majority stayed mostly the same.
